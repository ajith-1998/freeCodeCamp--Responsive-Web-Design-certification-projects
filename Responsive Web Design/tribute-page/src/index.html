Jon Stewart is a comedian who hosted the popular program "The Daily Show" on Comedy Central. A brilliant satirist, Jon routinely held up the absurdities in our daily lives for examination. Holding a special place in his heart for absurdities in the American political field, Jon routinely skewered politicians on both sides of the aisle, pointing out their hypocrisy and inconsistencies.

Notable guests on The Daily Show include:

  • President Barack Obama
  • Vice President Joe Biden
  • Former Presidnt Bill Clinton
  • Former Vice President Al Gore
  • Former Secretary of State and Former First Lady Hillary Clinton
  • Former President Jimmy Carter
  • Former UK Prime Minister Tony Blair
  • Former UK Prime Minister Gordon Brown
  • Numerous Heads of State, U.S. Cabinet members, U.S. Congresspersons, U.S. Governors and other political figures

Throughout his career, Jon has been nominated for over 100 awards. He more recent awards include:

  • 2016 - Peabody Award
  • 2015 - Critics' Choice Television Award for Best Talk Show
  • 2015 - Outstanding Variety Talk Series
  • 2014 - Freedom of Expression Award
  • 2013 - Outstanding Producer of Live Entertainment & Talk Television

Jon currently owns a 12 acre farm in Middletown, New Jersey, with his wife, Tracey. They use the farm as a sanctuary for abused animals.
